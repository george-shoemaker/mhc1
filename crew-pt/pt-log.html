25FEB2025 - Ability Run

Went smoothly. I assembled everyone in the gym first. Assigned groups and described the parameters

  1. This is not a race
  2. Keep your group together. Don't be a hero and blow up your group in the first mile.
  3. Turn around at 20 minutes. Pace yourselves such that you can return in 20 minutes.

40 min run results:

A: 4.5mi / 8:50 pace
B: 4.2mi / 9:30 pace
C: 3.4 mi / 11:25

I am happy with these paces. I sent out group A, then B, then C, which means groups would pass each other towards the end. This created a bit of a competitive climate which I think increased the paces slightly which is fine. If people want to compete, they will get their chance during the next 2 mile time trial.

03JAN2025 - 1 rep max

Max Strength assessment

Hammack's self assessment:

Sustains:

  • First formation dismissal: gave time to prep gym
  • Technique instruction and how to safely fail a lift: Gave people confidence to try their best while avoiding injury
  • Non-formal "coaching" approach: Allowed Hammack to engage with crew in an approachable, encouraging way. Environment is non judgemental and safe for everyone.
  • Shoemaker acting as scribe: Allowed Hammack to give people attention as needed.

Improves

• Elaborate on the the importance of rest between warm ups / sets / 1 rep max attempts

  • Better crowd control: Some time spent in "bro session" mode, Bravo entered the gym and side tracked Alpha.
  • A more in depth class on technique prior to the session: Would cut down the time to complete the assessment

02JAN2025 - 2 mile tt

Aerobic Fitness assessment

2mi-results

I estimate the course is 10 sec slower than a track (slope, hard turn around). Placed pink flagging at turn around.

Before the assessment I asked people their “best guess” for a 2 mile time for two reasons:

  1. Hammack thought self-reported times would overestimate performance. This was largely correct - average guess was 10% faster than recorded time.
  2. My hope was that by stating a goal time, people would take the assessment more seriously and try to hit that time.
