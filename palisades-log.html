08JAN2025

Diverted from Sanborn Project and returned to station for final gear check. Buggy change. Wheels rolling 1400. Plan is to rendezvous with Nevada City Crew in Tracy. We do not, but we link up with more CalFire leadership.

As of this date are the 3 main fires and their acreage:

  • Hurst : 0.7k acres
  • Eaton : 10.6k acres
  • Palisades : 11.8k acres

2330 drove west bound on Sunset blvd, past Station 69[4-1] which was burned over. Majority of structures burned down in this area. I believe the station is still standing, couldn't really see in the dark.

Turned south to the ocean on Temescal Canyon Rd, then west on Highway 1. Burnt down homes and businesses, smokes.

Palisades acreage has exceeded 17k acres. Confluence of factors (fuel load, drought, low humidity, primarily strong winds exceeding 50 mph).

There is less wind than previous days which makes offensive strategies possible.

Structure defense and reinforcing the creek in Topanga Canyon below Alma Rd. Fire has crept down to the creek and is up on the ridge, keeps flaring up then backing up slope.

Reassigned to cut line through vegetation along the backs of houses (WUI). Area is too steep for dozer. We are within 100 yards of flames down slope. TODO: 09JAN2025 gps. Compare with fire perimeter: did fire reach line? Did it jump line?

Return to trail head (briefing location). Sharpen tools and rehab water. Bed down at 21:30

10JAN2025

0300 wake up to widen line behind houses, wind pick up. Goal is to create a sufficient fire break to save the houses without question.

0730 leave for ICP, chow, rehab.

Sleeper trailers at Zuma Beach.

11JAN2025

Staging, 1030 assigned to further improve line from last shift. Fire has not reached this line yet. Reassigned to cut line around the black on top of hill (TODO: gps) still hot area. Work until we are out of fuel Rehab at buggy, 1600

1700 structure defense in WUI neighborhood in Topanga (TODO gps). Two civilians are filming us remove vegetation around houses, not sure of their intention. Nearly everyone is gone, evac zone. Done at 2130. Rehab, bed down

14JAN2025

0545 wheels rolling return to yeterday's staging, arrive at ICP[1-2] 0730 chow

1130 wheels rolling to hotel in downtown LA. Urban S&R is picking through burnt down homes near LAFD Station 70[2-1]. I imagine the fire was throwing embers south and it was luck of the draw whose roof succumbed to fire.

Tons of military vehicles staged at Will Rogers Beach[6] parking lot off Highway 1

Palisades Fire has remained at 23.7k acres since 12JAN2025, this thing pretty much done. Containment is up to 18%. There is still a red flag warning in effect with some risk that winds pick up and a new fire makes a run, but this seems more unlikely by the day.
